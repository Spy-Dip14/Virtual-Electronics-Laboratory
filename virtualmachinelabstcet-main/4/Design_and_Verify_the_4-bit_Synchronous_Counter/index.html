Instructions
Step 1: Press Switch to supply 5V to the Circuit.
Step 2: Press Counter button to start the counter.
  • if connection is correct, an alert appers. Click ok. Now, go to step 4.
  • if connection is wrong, an alert appers. Click ok. Now, go to step 3.
    • Step 3: Check the connection, if incorrect, click on node number to remove any particular connection(s) or click on reset button and start from step 1.
    Step 4: Different Combination of LEDs lit up for different combiation of Input.The data is simultaneously added to the truth table.
    Step 5: Repeat steps 2 to 4 for another 15 sets of data.
    Step 6: Click "Generate Waveform" button to generate the timing diagram.
    Step 7: Click "Print" to get the print out of the truth table and the timing diagram.
